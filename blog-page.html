For AEC firms in USA, the future of building design and construction is not just bricks & mortar – it is about bits and bytes. At the intersection of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and the Internet of Things (IoT) is born a powerful combination – transforming how we conceive, manage, and experience buildings. This convergence is no longer a futuristic concept – it is a real trend gaining momentum rapidly because of the demand for smarter, safer, more efficient, and occupant-centric spaces.
