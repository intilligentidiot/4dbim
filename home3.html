What We Deliver

Phasing Simulation Video

High-definition animated videos visualizing the entire construction timeline from excavation to handover, perfect for investor presentations.

Planned vs. Actual Analysis

Side-by-side visual comparisons of the baseline schedule versus on-site progress to instantly track delays and adjust workflows.

Time-Based Clash Reports

Identification of conflicts that only occur during specific timeframes, such as equipment moving through areas where ducts are being installed.

Logistics & Safety Plans

Detailed visual layouts for crane radiuses, temporary storage, scaffolding, and emergency access routes per construction phase.
